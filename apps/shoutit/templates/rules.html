{% trans "Trust and Safety" %}

{% trans "Participating in the Shoutit marketplace involves real people and real stuff. It's essential to protect yourself and use common sense, just like any other time you do business with someone new." %}

{#

Buyers: Don't pay until you get it.

#} {#
    #} {#
  • Is the item or service what you asked for? Does it work? Is it complete? Make sure you are satisfied before you complete the transaction.
    • #} {#
  • All sales are final and once you click "Pay" or hand over cash there's no going back, so be please be diligent!
    • #} {#
#} {#

Sellers: Make sure you get paid on the spot.

#} {#
    #} {#
  • If you are being paid by credit card through Shoutit, wait for the buyer to press "Pay" and wait to receive an SMS text message from Shoutit and/or a notice in the Shoutit application informing you that the payment has in fact been sent.
    • #} {#
  • If you are sent a payment via credit card, you will receive an email inviting you to create a PoundPay account (or you may use an existing account) in order to receive the funds. PoundPay is a third-party payment processing service used by Shoutit for credit card transactions.
    • #} {#
  • Due to risks of fraud, we do not recommend accepting checks, money orders or other methods of payment that may be forged or counterfeited. It's best to stick to credit card via the Shoutit application or cash.
    • #} {#
#}

{% trans "Safety First" %}

  • {% trans "Always meet in a public place, like a café or bank. Avoid doing large transactions in cash." %}
  • {% trans "Take care to protect your personal information, such as your name, address, phone number, credit card number. Only provide your personal information to the secure Shoutit application." %}

  • Only accept payment by credit card through Shoutit or in cash. Don't accept checks, money orders or other methods of payment.
