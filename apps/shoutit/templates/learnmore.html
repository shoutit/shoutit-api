{% trans "ABOUT SHOUTIT" %}

{% trans "Shoutit is the region’s first social marketplace where people shout their requests for goods or services that they need and the amount they would like to pay for them. It’s a way for you to connect with others in their local community on a fun and easy to use platform. Shoutit takes the concept of commerce back to the basics, by allowing you to log in and trade whatever you are offering or requesting with those around you." %}

{% trans "HOW TO POST A SHOUT" %}

{% trans "All you need to do is log in to your profiles so you can post shouts which offer or request an item or service, and then specify the amount you would like to buy or sell it for. And then it’s out there. Within moments, other users within the local community will connect with the shout, posting their own responses." %}

{% trans "LISTENING TO PEOPLE" %}

{% trans "Connect with old and new friends by listening to their shouts. Listen to other users by visiting their profile page and clicking on the listen icon so that their shout posts will be given priority on your browse page. If there is something specific you are searching for, you can also listen to your preferred tags or key words, in the same way you would listen to another user, to prioritize these shouts too." %}

{% trans "HOW TAGS WORK" %}

{% trans "When you are filling out your shout post, tags will be auto completed. You can also add your own tags by manually entering them in the tag box below the post. Tagging shouts enables them to be categorized and makes it easier to filter posts when searching." %}

{% trans "HOW TO SEARCH FOR A SHOUT" %}

{% trans "Searching is straight forward on the browse page and just enter your preferred keywords to filter through all the shouts to make sure you find what you are looking for. Although, if you do not have anything specific in mind, you can browse through all the shouts being posted within your local community on the browse page. You can also order the results by time, distance or leave it for us to give you best matches." %}

{% trans "INSTANT MESSAGING" %}

{% trans "Only the shouts you have posted will appear on your profile page. If another user answers your shout, this will appear in your inbox. This is where all of your conversations with other users will be stored. All conversations on Shoutit happen in real time. If both shouter and replier are online, they will be able to instant message each other on the conversations page, or below the shout itself." %}

{% trans "REPLYING TO A SHOUT" %}

{% trans "If you see a shout that you would like to respond to, you can post a reply by clicking on the shout to open it up into its own page. There will be a box below the shout where you can type your response. Your reply will then appear on the user’s inbox as well as your own to help you keep track of your interactions." %}

{% trans "ACTIVATING YOUR ACCOUNT" %}

{% trans "Once you have registered your information you will be sent an authorization email to confirm you are who you say you are. This email will contain a link back to the Shoutit site which you will need to click to verify your account. Once this step is complete, you are ready to shout! If you don’t verify your account, you will not be able to post more than 5 shouts or reply to others’ shouts – so make sure you follow this step to avoid missing out on being part of the action." %}
